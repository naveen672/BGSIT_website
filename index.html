About BGSIT

Welcome to BGS Institute of Technology

A prestigious institution offering world-class education in engineering and technology

Our Legacy

Established in 2005, BGSIT is located on a lush 21-acre campus on the Bengaluru-Hassan-Mangaluru National Highway (NH-75). We are strategically positioned, approximately 80 kilometers from major cities like Bengaluru, Mysuru, Mandya, Hassan, and Tumkur.

As a proud member of Sri Adichunchanagiri Shikshana Trust, we are committed to creating tomorrow's engineers through quality education and strong ethical values.
